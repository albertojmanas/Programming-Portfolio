A little bit about me...

Originally from Long Island, NY. I have been all around the United States as well as overseas. Music is an absolute passion of mine and I couldn't imaging where I would be without it. Since the humble beginnings of recording bands in my parents computer room at 15 years old, I have been involved in numerous records as a producer, recording and mixing engineer. Recording, mixing, mastering & producing for artists/bands such as Asher Roth & UNITYTX, and have made it a point to be involved with as many creatives in my community as possible.
